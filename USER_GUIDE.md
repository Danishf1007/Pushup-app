## Getting Started

### 1. Registration

1. **Open the App** - Launch PushUp on your device
2. **Click "Sign Up"** on the welcome screen
3. **Choose Your Role:**
   - **Athlete** - If you want to train and track your workouts
   - **Coach** - If you want to manage athletes and create training plans
4. **Fill in Your Details:**
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (must contain: 6+ characters, 1 uppercase letter, 1 number)
   - Confirm Password
5. **For Athletes:** Optionally enter your Coach Code to link with your coach
6. **Accept Terms** and click "Create Account"

### 2. Login

1. Click **"Login"** on the welcome screen
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**

> **Note:** Invalid credentials will show an error message. Make sure your email and password are correct.

## How to Use - Athlete

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard shows:
- **Today's Workout** - Your scheduled activity for today
- **Current Streak** - Days in a row you've worked out
- **Progress This Week** - Weekly workout summary
- **Quick Stats** - Total workouts, active days, and achievements

### Viewing Your Training Plan

1. Go to **"Workouts"** tab
2. See your assigned training plan
3. Click on an activity to view details:
   - Activity name and type
   - Duration and sets/reps
   - Instructions from your coach

### Logging a Workout

1. **From Dashboard:**
   - Click **"Start Workout"** on today's activity
   
   **OR**
   
   **From Workouts Tab:**
   - Select an activity from your plan
   - Click **"Log Workout"**

2. **Fill in Workout Details:**
   - Duration (required)
   - Distance (if applicable)
   - Effort Level (1-10)
   - Notes (optional)

3. Click **"Save"**

> **Tip:** Logging workouts maintains your streak and updates your statistics!

### Tracking Progress

1. Go to **"Progress"** tab
2. View:
   - **Weekly Chart** - Workout frequency over time
   - **Activity Breakdown** - Types of exercises completed
   - **Recent Activity** - Your workout history
   - **Statistics** - Total workouts, duration, and active days

### Viewing Achievements

1. Go to **"Achievements"** tab (trophy icon)
2. See all available achievements
3. Track your progress toward locked achievements
4. Celebrate unlocked achievements! üéâ

### Messaging Your Coach

1. Go to **"Profile"** tab
2. Click **"Message Coach"** (if you have a coach)
3. Type your message and send
4. View message history in the conversation

## How to Use - Coach

### Dashboard Overview
Your coach dashboard shows:
- **Active Athletes** - Total athletes you're coaching
- **Active Plans** - Number of training plans
- **Athletes List** - Your team members with activity status
- **Training Plans** - Quick access to your plans
- **Recent Activity** - Latest workouts from your athletes

### Managing Athletes

1. **View All Athletes:**
   - Go to **"Athletes"** tab
   - See all athletes you're coaching

2. **View Athlete Details:**
   - Click on an athlete's card
   - See their:
     - Current streak
     - Total workouts
     - Assigned plans
     - Recent activity

3. **Link New Athletes:**
   - Share your **Coach ID** (found in Profile)
   - Athletes enter your ID during registration
   - They'll automatically link to your account

### Creating Training Plans

1. **Navigate to Plans:**
   - Click **"Plans"** tab
   - Click **"+ Create Plan"**

2. **Fill in Plan Details:**
   - **Plan Name** - e.g., "Beginner Strength Program"
   - **Description** - What the plan includes
   - **Duration** - Number of weeks
   - **Fitness Level** - Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
   - **Category** - Strength, Cardio, Flexibility, etc.

3. **Add Activities:**
   - Click **"+ Add Activity"**
   - Enter:
     - Activity Name (e.g., "Push-ups")
     - Type (Strength, Cardio, etc.)
     - Duration/Sets/Reps
     - Instructions
   - Click **"Add"**
   - Repeat for all activities in the plan

4. **Save Plan:**
   - Review all activities
   - Click **"Create Plan"**

### Assigning Plans to Athletes

1. **From Athlete Detail Screen:**
   - Go to **Athletes** tab
   - Click on an athlete
   - Click **"Assign Plan"**
   - Select a plan from your list
   - Choose start date
   - Confirm assignment

2. **From Plan Detail Screen:**
   - Go to **Plans** tab
   - Click on a plan
   - Click **"Assign to Athlete"**
   - Select athlete(s)
   - Confirm

### Monitoring Progress

1. **View Team Analytics:**
   - Check dashboard **Recent Activity** section
   - See who's been active recently

2. **Individual Athlete Progress:**
   - Go to athlete detail screen
   - View their:
     - Workouts completed
     - Current streak
     - Assigned plans progress

### Messaging Athletes

1. **From Athlete Detail:**
   - Click on athlete
   - Click message icon
   - Type and send message

2. **From Messages Tab:**
   - View all conversations
   - Click on a conversation
   - Send messages

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login

**Error: "Invalid email or password"**
- Double-check your email is spelled correctly
- Verify your password (remember: case-sensitive)
- Try the "Forgot Password" option if you can't remember

**Error: "Network error"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few moments

### Activity Not Saving

- Ensure all required fields are filled (duration)
- Check you have an active internet connection
- Make sure you're assigned to the activity's plan

### Coach ID Not Working

- Verify you copied the full Coach ID
- Check for extra spaces
- Confirm with your coach that the ID is correct
- You can also link after registration by asking your coach to assign you

### Not Seeing Recent Workouts

- Pull down on the screen to refresh
- Check that workouts were logged with valid data
- Ensure you're connected to the internet

### Streak Not Updating

- Workouts must be logged on the same day they're completed
- Streak counts consecutive days with at least one workout
- Missing a day resets your streak to 0

### Messages Not Sending

- Check internet connection
- Verify you're linked with the coach/athlete
- Try logging out and back in
